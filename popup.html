Please enable panels

Before you can open Black Menu, or parts of Black Menu in a panel, you need to enable panels. To do that, follow these steps.

1. Go to

chrome://flags

2. Locate the 'Enable Panels' panel

3. Enable the 'Enable Panels' flag

4. Relaunch Chrome

5. Done

Define a shortcut key to open Black Menu

Follow these steps to define a shortcut key to open black menu.

1. Go to

chrome://extensions

2. Scroll all the way to the bottom of the page

3. Click on 'Configure Commands'

4. Locate Black Menu

5. Click the 'not set' box next to Black Menu

6. Press the key you want to attach to Black Menu

7. Click OK

8. Done
